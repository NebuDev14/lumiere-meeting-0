# Background

- \- You should have **some** technical background related to the company you intern for
- \- If possible, find the tech stack!
- \- Research the company and field, finding industry-standard technologies
- \- These differ depending on the type of company and field!

# Mindset üíØüôè

> If you are overqualified for an internship,
> you are wasting your time.
> Never feel the need to be "good enough"
> for a position. If you have any matching experience,
> just go for it!

# Approach for internship-hunting

<ul>
  <li>
    - Well-researched & exceptional application >>> standard applications to 50
    companies
  </li>
  <li>- Bank on an open-minded CEO</li>
  <li>- Be incredibly polite and concise</li>
</ul>
